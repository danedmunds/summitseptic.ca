About Us :

Summit Septic Services provides all your septic and holding tank emptying needs. We serve Minnedosa,MB and the surrounding area. Frank Ford's extensive experience in waste management provides him with the knowledge and expertise to handle any of your septic needs.


Please Contact Us for more information or to receive a quote.

Our Services

Homes

We empty septic tanks and holding tanks for residences that are not on the town sewage system.

Cabins and Cottages

We provide sewage removal services for the cabin and cottage communities surrounding Minnedosa Beach and area.

RVs and Motorhomes

Finally got that large RV or motorhome parked in the perfect spot? No need to go offsite to have your holding tank emptied. We can remove it right there so that you don't have to maneuver in and out of busy campgrounds.

Rural Service

We provide services to Minnedosa and the surrounding farms and communities. Give us a call to see if you are located within our service boundaries.
